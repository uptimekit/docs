UptimeKit supports various integrations to keep you notified about your service status. You can configure these integrations to receive alerts when incidents are created, updated, or resolved.

## Available Integrations

Currently, the following integrations are supported:

- Discord: Receive incident notifications directly in your Discord channels.
- Telegram: Get notified in your Telegram channel when incidents occur.
- Webhook: Send raw JSON payloads to any custom endpoint for flexible integration with other systems.

## Managing Integrations

To manage your integrations, navigate to Settings > Integrations in your dashboard. From there, you can view available integrations, configure new ones, and manage existing connections.
