UptimeKit is an open-source, modern, and powerful status page and monitoring solution. It allows you to monitor your services, display their status to your users, and manage incidents effectively.

## Key Features

- **Uptime Monitoring**: Track service uptime with HTTP/HTTPS, TCP, ICMP Ping, Keyword, and HTTP JSON monitors
- **Status Pages**: Beautiful, customizable status pages with optional password protection and custom domains
- **Monitor Organization**: Use groups and tags to organize monitors, with automatic SSL certificate tracking
- **Incident Management**: Create, update, and manage incidents to communicate service status
- **Integrations**: Connect with Discord, Telegram, Prometheus AlertManager, and custom webhooks for automatic incident creation
- **Team Collaboration**: Invite team members to manage status pages and monitors
- **Open Source**: Self-hostable and fully extensible
