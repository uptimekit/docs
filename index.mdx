UptimeKit is an open-source, modern, and powerful status page and monitoring solution. It allows you to monitor your services, display their status to your users, and manage incidents effectively.

## Key Features

- Uptime Monitoring: Keep track of your services' uptime with support for HTTP, TCP, and other protocols.
- Status Pages: Beautiful, customizable status pages to keep your users informed.
- Incident Management: Create and manage incidents to communicate outages and maintenance.
- Team Collaboration: Invite team members to manage your status pages and monitors.
- Open Source: Self-hostable and fully extensible.
